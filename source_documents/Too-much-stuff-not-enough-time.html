Tags: connectedcourses, edu8117

The plan by now was that I would have spent a few weeks engaging with the readings from NETGL and figuring out what they can offer some insights for enhancing and transforming the two courses I currently teach. Time for me to feel a little of their pain and see just how well I can handle it.
