Limitations of incoming students and Gantt charts

Tags: bam, Uncategorized

Thought it would be a grand idea to get the students, in week 3, to prepare a Gantt chart planning out their study for the term. They get to apply some content knowledge and think actively about planning their study. Of course, the problem is that many of these students are new to technology. Satish is reporting that a number of the students have never used computers and don't have an email address. They are struggling with getting going with the tools and also the structure/format of Gantt charts. Will have to re-think this one for next time.
